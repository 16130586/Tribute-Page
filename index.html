Life History

  • Shinkai studied Japanese literature at Chuo University

  • He is married to retired actress and producer Chieko Misaka, star of films including Versus by Ryuhei Kitamura; they have a daughter, child actress Chise Niitsu

  • 1994, He got a job at Falcom after He have graduated

  • 1999, Shinkai released She and Her Cat, a five-minute short piece done in monochrome. It won several awards, including the grand prize at the 12th DoGA CG Animation contest (2000)

  • In June 2000, Shinkai was inspired to begin Voices of a Distant Star by drawing a picture of a girl in a cockpit grasping a cell phone

  • In May 2001, he quit his job at Falcom and began to work on Voices

  • November 20, 2004. The Place Promised in Our Early Days, which was released nationwide in Japan

  • He completed 5 Centimeters per Second

  • May 31, 2013. The Garden of Words, was released

  • In August 26, 2016, Shinkai released Your Name. The film received critical acclaim, was praised for its animation and emotional impact, and was also a commercial success.
